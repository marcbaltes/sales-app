    The Care Needs Score
    This score is calculated based on data compiled for the last week using a variety of data points from different sources including: Admission/Discharge/Transfer messages, Resident Demographics, COMS Assessment data elements (including ADLs) and Medications (when available) and using a algorhythms based on the typical time spent providing these tasks within a day at a skilled nursing facility.
